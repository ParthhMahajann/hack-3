📚 Risk Scoring Methodology
▼ expand
All thresholds are derived from peer-reviewed clinical guidelines:
  • WHO ANC 2016 — "WHO recommendations on antenatal care for a positive pregnancy experience" (WHO/RHR/16.12) — Maternal BP, Hb, GDM thresholds
  • MOHFW IMNCI India 2009 — "Integrated Management of Neonatal and Childhood Illness" — Child danger signs, fever thresholds
  • WHO Child Growth Standards 2006 — "WHO Multicentre Growth Reference Study Group" — Weight-for-age Z-score (LMS method)
  • WHO MUAC 2013 — "Pocket book of hospital care for children" — SAM <115mm, MAM 115–125mm
  • FOGSI 2020 — "High-risk pregnancy guidelines" — Adolescent/elderly maternal age
  • IDF/WHO GDM Criteria — Gestational diabetes FBS threshold >126 mg/dL
  • JSY/JSSK MOHFW 2015 — Incentive amounts per event (₹1400 rural delivery, etc.)
